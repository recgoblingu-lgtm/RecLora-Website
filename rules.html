📜 RecLora Community Rules

Welcome to RecLora! Our goal is to create a safe, welcoming, and enjoyable experience for everyone. By playing RecLora or participating in any official RecLora community, you agree to follow these rules.

1. Be Respectful

Treat everyone with respect and kindness. Harassment, bullying, discrimination, hate speech, threats, or targeted abuse are not allowed.

2. Keep Content Appropriate

Usernames, profile pictures, room names, inventions, and all user-created content must remain appropriate. NSFW, hateful, excessively violent, or offensive content is prohibited.

3. No Cheating or Exploiting

Using cheats, hacks, modified clients, exploits, macros, or any unauthorized software to gain an unfair advantage is prohibited. If you discover a bug, report it instead of abusing it.

4. No Impersonation

Do not impersonate RecLora developers, moderators, staff members, Rec Room staff, or other players.

5. No Spam or Advertising

Spamming, flooding chat, or advertising unrelated servers, websites, products, or services without permission is prohibited.

6. Protect Your Account

Never share your password or account information. You are responsible for everything that happens on your account.

7. Respect Staff

Follow instructions from moderators and administrators. If you disagree with a decision, use the proper appeal process respectfully instead of arguing publicly.

8. No Ban Evasion

Creating or using another account to bypass a suspension or ban is prohibited and may result in permanent removal.

9. Respect Other Players

Do not intentionally ruin another player's experience through griefing, trolling, or repeated disruptive behavior.

10. Report Bugs Responsibly

If you discover bugs, exploits, or security issues, report them to the RecLora team instead of abusing or publicly sharing them.

11. Use Common Sense

Not every situation can be covered by these rules. If your actions negatively affect other players or the community, staff may take appropriate action.

12. Enforcement

Violations of these rules may result in warnings, temporary mutes, temporary suspensions, permanent bans, removal of content, or additional disciplinary action depending on the severity of the violation.

📌 Reminder

Also make sure to follow the Rec Room Code of Conduct while using RecLora. Following both the RecLora Community Rules and the Rec Room Code of Conduct helps create a safe, welcoming, and enjoyable experience for everyone.
