📜 RecLora Community Rules

Welcome to RecLora! Our goal is to provide a fun, safe, and welcoming experience for everyone. By using RecLora, you agree to follow these rules.

1. Be Respectful

Treat all players with kindness and respect. Harassment, bullying, discrimination, hate speech, threats, or targeted abuse are not allowed.

2. Keep Content Appropriate

Do not create, upload, or share content that is sexually explicit, excessively violent, hateful, or otherwise inappropriate. Usernames, profile pictures, room names, and creations must also remain appropriate.

3. No Cheating or Exploiting

Using cheats, modified clients, hacks, exploits, macros, or unauthorized software to gain an unfair advantage is prohibited. If you discover a bug, report it instead of abusing it.

4. No Impersonation

Do not impersonate RecLora developers, moderators, staff members, or other players. Claiming to have official authority when you do not is prohibited.

5. No Spam or Advertising

Spamming messages, flooding chats, or advertising unrelated servers, websites, products, or services without permission is not allowed.

6. Protect Your Account

Keep your account secure. Never share your password or account information with anyone. You are responsible for everything that happens on your account.

7. Respect Staff

Follow instructions from moderators and administrators. If you disagree with a decision, remain respectful and use the proper appeal process instead of arguing publicly.

8. No Ban Evasion

Creating or using another account to avoid a suspension or ban is prohibited and may result in permanent removal from RecLora.

9. Respect Other Players

Do not intentionally ruin another player's experience through griefing, trolling, excessive disruption, or repeated unwanted behavior.

10. Report Issues Responsibly

If you encounter bugs, exploits, security issues, or rule-breaking players, report them to the RecLora team. Do not abuse or publicly share exploits.

11. Use Common Sense

Not every situation can be covered by these rules. If your actions negatively impact the community or the game, staff may take appropriate action to protect the experience for everyone.

12. Enforcement

Violations of these rules may result in warnings, temporary mutes, temporary suspensions, permanent bans, removal of content, or other actions deemed necessary by the RecLora staff team. Enforcement is determined based on the severity and frequency of violations.

📌 Reminder

Also make sure to follow the Rec Room Code of Conduct while using RecLora. Following both the RecLora Community Rules and the Rec Room Code of Conduct helps create a safe, welcoming, and enjoyable experience for everyone.
