RecLora Rules

Please read these rules before playing or participating in the community.

1. Be Respectful

Treat all players with respect. Harassment, bullying, discrimination, threats, and hate speech are not allowed.

2. No Cheating or Exploiting

Using cheats, exploits, modified clients, or unauthorized software to gain an unfair advantage is prohibited.

3. Keep Content Appropriate

Do not create or share NSFW, excessively violent, or otherwise inappropriate content.

4. No Impersonation

Do not pretend to be a RecLora staff member, developer, moderator, or another player.

5. No Spam or Advertising

Do not spam messages, repeatedly advertise, or promote unrelated servers, websites, or projects without permission.

6. Report Bugs Responsibly

If you discover a bug or exploit, report it to the development team instead of abusing or sharing it.

7. Respect Staff Decisions

Follow instructions from moderators and administrators. If you believe a mistake was made, submit an appeal respectfully.

8. Protect Your Account

Keep your account secure. Never share your password or account information with anyone.

9. Use Common Sense

If something seems harmful, disruptive, or unfair to other players, don't do it. Help keep RecLora welcoming for everyone.

10. Have Fun!

RecLora is built for players to create, explore, and enjoy together. Be friendly, make new friends, and have fun!

Reminder

Also make sure to follow the Rec Room Code of Conduct while using RecLora. Following both the RecLora Rules and the Rec Room Code of Conduct helps keep the community safe, respectful, and enjoyable for everyone.
